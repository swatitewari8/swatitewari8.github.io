About me

My name is Swati Tewari and was born and brought up in Kanpur city, in India, which is famous for its leather and textile industries. I am currently in my first year of the Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) program from the university of Ottawa, Canada. I am a teacher candidate of year 2023. I am in the Intermediate/Senior (I/S) division and my teachable subjects are Chemistry and Mathematics. Learning has been my passion and I have been a continuous life-long learner. I hold a doctoral degree (PhD) in Organic Chemistry from Central Drug Research Institute, India; a Master of Science (MSc) degree in Chemistry from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kanpur, India; Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Chemistry, Physics and Mathematics from India and an MBA degree from University of Alberta, Edmonton, Canada. I have taught as a professor in Sheridan College, Brampton, Canada; Dawson College, Montréal, Canada and Amity University, Noida, India. Some of the subjects that I taught at the undergraduate and graduate levels include Chemistry, Physics for Chemical Sciences, Environmental Sciences, Introductory Mathematics and various courses in Management domain. I have also worked as a research scientist and research manager in pharmaceutical industries in Canada. My other professional engagements include having worked as a post doctoral fellow at the Max-Planck Institute of Molecular Physiology, Dortmund, Germany and City University of New York, NY, USA. My research interest areas are spread across drug discovery and design as antiparasitic, antibacterial and pain killer agents. I have published several research articles in peer reviewed journals.
